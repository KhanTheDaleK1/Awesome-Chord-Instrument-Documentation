🏛️ History

From the ancient Sumerian Harp to the electronic legacy of Suzuki's 1981 OM-27, chord instruments have evolved to provide the harmonic "glue" for musicians worldwide.

The Suzuki legacy began in 1953, with the Omnichord debuting in 1981 as a revolutionary consumer novelty that became an underground cult classic.
