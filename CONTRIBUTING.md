# Contributing to Awesome Chord Instruments

We love your input! We want to make contributing to this project as easy and transparent as possible.

## 2026 Awesome Standards: Contribution Maturity

To ensure the list remains a high-signal, high-quality knowledge base, we enforce a strict **Contribution Maturity** rule:

- **30-Day Rule**: Any new resource (article, tool, video, repository) must be at least **30 days old** and demonstrate a minimum level of community activity or stability before being submitted. 
- Please do not submit newly published posts or newly created repositories as they have not yet proven their value over time.

## Structured Resource Entry Format

When submitting a pull request, ensure your entry follows our strict schema:

`[Clean Title](URL) ![Badge](BadgeURL) - One concise sentence explaining why it’s awesome. #Tag1 #Tag2`

*Example:*
`[Omnichord 101 - History & Technique](https://theproaudiofiles.com/suzuki-omnichord/) ![status-stable](https://img.shields.io/badge/status-stable-green) - Comprehensive breakdown of history, strumplate interface, and analog circuit translation. #History #Tutorial`

Please add your entry to the appropriate Diátaxis category: Tutorials, How-To Guides, Reference, or Explanation.

## Commits

We prefer Conventional Commits (e.g., `feat: add new emulator link`, `fix: update broken URL`).
